Admission Information

Thank you for considering GGSIPU for your education. Below, you'll find information about the admission process and eligibility criteria.

Admission Process

The admission process at GGSIPU is designed to be transparent and merit-based. Prospective students are required to follow these steps:

  1. Review the admission requirements on the official website.
  2. Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for the desired program.
  3. Complete and submit the online application form.
  4. Pay the application fee as specified in the admission guidelines.
  5. Attend any required entrance exams or interviews (if applicable).
  6. Receive admission offer and complete the enrollment process.

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility criteria vary by program. Please refer to the official admission brochure or contact the admission office for specific details regarding the program you are interested in.
