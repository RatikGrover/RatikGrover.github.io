BA LLB

Introduction

The Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Laws (BA LLB) program at Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) offers a comprehensive education in both humanities and law. This integrated program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in legal studies alongside a broad understanding of arts and humanities.

Curriculum Overview

Humanities Courses

The BA LLB curriculum includes a range of humanities courses, including subjects such as literature, history, political science, and sociology. These courses contribute to a well-rounded education, enriching students' understanding of societal and cultural contexts.

Legal Studies

Legal studies in the program cover various aspects of law, including constitutional law, contracts, family law, and more. This ensures that students develop a strong legal foundation alongside their studies in the humanities.

Practical Exposure

Practical exposure is an integral part of the BA LLB program. Moot court sessions, internships with law firms, and legal clinics provide students with hands-on experience, allowing them to apply legal concepts and theories in real-world situations.
