BBA LLB

Introduction

The Bachelor of Business Administration and Bachelor of Laws (BBA LLB) program at Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) combines business and legal education to prepare students for a career at the intersection of law and business. This integrated program provides a comprehensive understanding of both disciplines.

Curriculum Overview

Business Courses

The BBA LLB curriculum at GGSIPU includes core business courses such as principles of management, marketing, finance, and business ethics. These courses lay the foundation for understanding the business aspect of legal issues.

Legal Studies

Legal studies in the program cover various aspects of law, including constitutional law, contracts, criminal law, and more. This ensures that students develop a strong legal foundation alongside their business education.

Practical Exposure

Practical exposure is a key component of the BBA LLB program. Moot court sessions, internships with law firms, and business simulations provide students with real-world experience, allowing them to apply legal and business concepts in practice.
